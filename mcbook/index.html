Booking management

Customer database

Every person who books with you is saved to your customer list — name, email, and phone. Build a real relationship with your clients over time and see their booking history at a glance.

Smart availability

Set your working hours day by day. Block out specific dates for holidays or time off. Set a minimum notice period so customers can only book slots with enough lead time for you to prepare.

Create as many services as you offer — each with its own name, duration, price, and payment mode. A haircut, a colour treatment, and a beard trim can all have completely different settings.

💰

Stripe payouts

Connect your Stripe account and receive payments directly into your bank. MCBook handles the checkout flow — you just show up for the appointment and get paid.

Payments

Collect payment, your way

Every service can have a different payment mode. Choose the one that makes sense for your business — or mix and match across services.

💳

Charge after appointment

The customer's card is saved securely at booking — nothing is charged yet. Once the appointment is done, you charge them the service fee from your bookings dashboard. A small platform fee applies on the transaction.

🚫

No-show fee protection

The customer saves their card at booking but is only ever charged if they don't show up. You set the no-show fee per service. If they do show up, you collect payment your way. A small platform fee applies only if the no-show fee is charged.

🆕

Not collected through website

No card is saved and no payment is taken online. Customers fill in their name, email, and phone and book a time. Payment is arranged directly in person or by invoice. Always free — no MCBook fee applies.
