Payments

Collect payment, your way

Every service can have a different payment mode. Choose the one that makes sense for your business — or mix and match across services.

💳

Charge after appointment

The customer's card is saved securely at booking — nothing is charged yet. Once the appointment is done, you charge them the service fee from your bookings dashboard. A 3% MCBook fee applies on the transaction.

🚫

No-show fee protection

The customer saves their card at booking but is only ever charged if they don't show up. You set the no-show fee per service. If they do show up, you collect payment your way. A 3% MCBook fee applies only if the no-show fee is charged.

🆕

Not collected through website

No card is saved and no payment is taken online. Customers fill in their name, email, and phone and book a time. Payment is arranged directly in person or by invoice. Always free — no MCBook fee applies.
